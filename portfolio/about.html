About me

My name is Tudor and currently I'm a student at Menlo School and play for the Menlo Tennis Varsity Team.

I've played Tennis since I was 6 years old. I started playing in Romania in a small club, before I moved to Germany and played at a bigger club, and I played tournaments all over Germany ultimately getting a sponsorship from Wilson.

Last year I moved to the US and switched to a new surface: hard-court. In the US, I play tournaments with my Highschool Team, but also travel indivually throughout the US.
I usually practice 10 hours a week at the Eagle Fustar Academy in Sunnyvale.
For those of you who are interested: I also give tennis lessons to young people to guide them through their tennis careers.
